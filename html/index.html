The Younger Lab Mosquito Management Database aims to aid lab members in the breeding of transgenic mosquitos, retrieval of mosquito line data, and maintenance of lab protocols. This webpage also provides diagnostic plotting of clutch egg quantities, and simplified data entry forms. A help page is provided to guide the user through the main functionalities of the database.

This project was completed by Dylan Tamayo, Cameron Vicnaire, Bhanu Shankar Dhulipalla, and Andreea Stoica as a project for BF768 Biological Database Analysis with Dr. Gary Benson, and under the supervision of Dr. Meg Younger.
